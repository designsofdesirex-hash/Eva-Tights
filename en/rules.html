The Rules

This isn't filler — it's how we keep things smooth for both of us. Read it once, then you're good.

01

No Unsolicited Pictures

Don't send photos I didn't ask for. Not in DMs, not in emails, not attached to booking requests. If I want to see something, I'll ask. This is non-negotiable.

02

No Calls Without Permission

Don't call unless we've agreed on a call beforehand. Message first — always. Unexpected calls will be ignored and repeated attempts will get you blocked.

03

Payment First — Always

Payment clears before any work begins. A secure payment link is sent once your request is confirmed. No 'I'll pay after,' no exceptions. Chargebacks without contacting me first end the relationship permanently.

04

Privacy Must Be Respected

What's between us stays between us. No sharing my content, no screenshotting conversations, no reposting anywhere. Every file I send is watermarked and traceable. If I find my work somewhere it shouldn't be, you're done.

05

Be Direct About What You Want

Tell me exactly what you're looking for — the outfit, the angle, the vibe, the length. The more specific you are, the better the result. Vague requests like 'surprise me' slow everything down and cost you more time.

06

Communication Windows

I reply within 24–48 hours. If you haven't heard back, wait. Don't send follow-ups, don't message on multiple platforms, don't try to 'check in.' Repeated pinging gets you blocked. VIP members get faster replies.

07

Break the Rules, Lose Access

Violate any of the above — redistribution, disrespect, boundary-pushing, payment games — and you're blocked permanently. No refund, no second chance, no discussion.
