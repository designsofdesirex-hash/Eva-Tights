What we use cookies for

  • Necessary (always on): remembering that you've passed the age gate, your theme preference (Verre / Peau / Écailles), and your Rules-agreement token. The site can't function as intended without these.
  • Analytics: we use a cookieless analytics tool (Plausible- or Fathom-style), which reports only aggregate, anonymous traffic numbers and doesn't set a tracking cookie or build an individual profile. Because it's cookieless, it doesn't require consent under most interpretations of ePrivacy law — but we still list it here for transparency.
  • Marketing: off by default. Only used if a retargeting pixel is added later (e.g. for a specific ad campaign), and only with your consent via the banner.
